All about Porsche

Porsche is one of the largest luxury and performance automotive companies in the world. They are known especially for their great performance. Many of their cars rival cars that cost twice or even three times as much on the track. The cars are designed carefully with driving capabilities in mind. Many of their cars handle like butter and are able to keep a great amount of grip on the track when at high speeds even through hard turns. Porsche was founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931.
